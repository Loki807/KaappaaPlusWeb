Kaappaan Safety App

Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

1. Introduction

Kaappaan is a safety and emergency support application developed to assist users across Sri Lanka with quick access to emergency contacts, alerts, and location-based help services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Location (only when user requests emergency help)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Account details (name, phone number if registered)
  • Usage logs (for app improvement)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide emergency response and connect with authorities
  • To show nearest police, fire, and ambulance services
  • To improve app security and performance
  • To notify users about critical safety alerts

4. Location Data Usage

Your location is accessed ONLY when: "Send Emergency Alert" is pressed. We do not track your live movement and do not share your location with third parties except emergency services when required.

5. Data Sharing Policy

Your data may be shared ONLY with:

  • Sri Lanka Police (119 / 118)
  • Fire & Rescue (110)
  • Suwa Seriya Ambulance (1990)
  • Disaster Response (117)

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your information to any third-party companies.

6. Fake Reports & Misuse

Submitting false emergency alerts is illegal in Sri Lanka. Users who intentionally misuse Kaappaan may face:

  • Temporary app suspension
  • Permanent account termination
  • Legal action by authorities

7. Security Measures

  • Data encryption (AES 256-bit)
  • Protected secure servers
  • Strict access control policies

8. User Rights

  • Right to request data deletion
  • Right to request data correction
  • Right to stop location sharing at any time

9. Consent

By using Kaappaan, you consent to our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
